Founder

Building practical cybersecurity for the real world

Pavan Kumar

Founder & Security Engineer — Secracy

I founded Secracy with a clear focus: help organizations defend against real-world cyber threats, not theoretical ones.

My background is rooted in computer applications, applied artificial intelligence, and hands-on offensive security. Over the years, I’ve worked closely with real systems, real vulnerabilities, and real incidents — experiences that shaped Secracy’s practical, attacker-minded approach to security.

I hold an OSCP certification, which reflects my emphasis on offensive security, penetration testing, and understanding systems from an attacker’s perspective. This mindset helps in designing defenses that actually work under pressure.

With a foundation in BCA and applied AI & Machine Learning in IIT Ropar, I’m particularly interested in how automation and intelligent analysis can reduce noise, improve detection, and support security teams without replacing human judgment.

Secracy is built to serve startups, growing teams, and enterprises that want clear, honest, and effective security — without unnecessary complexity or fear-driven decisions.

Core Principles

  • Security should be proactive, not reactive
  • Offensive knowledge strengthens defense
  • Automation should assist, not blind
  • Practical risk reduction matters more than checklists
